Privacy Policy for 54-Day Novena Tracker

Effective Date: September 1, 2025

Last Updated: September 1, 2025

1. Introduction

Woodley Solutions ("we," "our," or "us") operates the 54-Day Novena Tracker mobile application and website at 54dayrosary.com (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Service.

The 54-Day Novena Tracker is designed to help Catholics complete the traditional 54-day rosary novena with respect for your privacy and spiritual journey.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Prayer Intentions: Personal prayer requests you choose to enter
  • Progress Data: Which days of the novena you have completed
  • Notification Preferences: Your chosen reminder times and settings

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Analytics Data: Anonymous usage statistics through Google Analytics 4 (when analytics are enabled)
  • Technical Data: Device type, browser version, and basic performance metrics
  • No Personal Identification: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other personally identifying information

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Prayer Tracking: Store your novena progress and intentions locally on your device
  • Notifications: Send daily prayer reminders if you choose to enable them
  • App Improvement: Anonymous analytics help us improve the prayer experience
  • Technical Support: Troubleshoot issues and maintain service reliability

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

  • Your personal prayer data (intentions, progress) is stored locally on your device
  • We do not store your prayer intentions or personal spiritual information on our servers
  • You can clear all data through the app settings at any time

4.2 Security Measures

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • No storage of sensitive personal or spiritual information on external servers
  • Regular security updates to protect your privacy

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Google Analytics

  • We use Google Analytics 4 for anonymous usage statistics
  • Analytics only run in production environment
  • No personally identifiable information is shared with Google
  • You can opt out through your device's advertising settings

5.2 Ko-fi (Donation Platform)

  • Optional donation links redirect to Ko-fi's website
  • Ko-fi has its own privacy policy for any donations you choose to make
  • We do not receive or store your payment information

5.3 Netlify (Hosting)

  • Our website is hosted on Netlify
  • Standard web server logs may be collected by Netlify
  • Netlify's privacy policy applies to their data collection

6. Your Privacy Rights

  • Data Deletion: Clear all your data through app settings
  • Notification Control: Enable or disable prayer reminders at any time
  • Analytics Opt-Out: Use your device's privacy settings to limit analytics
  • No Account Required: Use the app without creating any accounts

7. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy. Changes are effective immediately upon posting.

9. International Users

This Service is operated from the United States. If you are accessing our Service from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States.
